## Equation of Everything

For me, **7 = 4**, but not in the way your math teacher would approve.

The **7** reminds me of perfection—not human perfection, because that doesn't exist. Only God is perfect. It's a daily reminder that perfection isn't something to achieve; it's something to admire.

So where does the **4** come from?

**4 = 1 + 3**

* **1** = One God (*Unus Deus*).
* **3** = Three *Personae*: **Pater**, **Filius**, and **Spiritus Sanctus**.

as the ancient Latin expression beautifully puts it: **Una Essentia, Tres Personae.**

In my symbolic equation, the One is revealed in the Three, and so 1 + 3 becomes 4..

And that's why **74** is special to me. it's a reminder that life isn't about becoming perfect. It's about remembering where perfection truly belongs.

Besides... every good equation deserves a beautiful constant.
